🌱 SEO — Organic Search
SEO was the foundation of the entire strategy. The website was built with keyword-rich content, optimised headings, alt text on all images, internal linking, and mobile-friendly design — following Google best practices from day one. Organic traffic consistently outperformed every paid channel and became the single biggest source of visitors throughout the campaign.

What Made the SEO Work
🔑 Keyword-Optimised Content
Every product page and landing page was written around high-intent keywords researched using Moz. Headings, descriptions, and CTAs all incorporated target terms naturally.
🔗 Internal Linking Structure
Product pages linked to each other and to the main catalogue, keeping users on-site longer and helping search engines understand the site structure.
📱 Mobile-First Design
The website was designed to be fully responsive across all devices — a critical factor in search engine rankings and user experience for the target demographics.
🖼️ Alt Text & Accessibility
All images included descriptive alt text — improving both search visibility and accessibility for users with disabilities, in line with WCAG guidelines.
💰 Paid Search Advertising (SEM)
Paid search ads were introduced mid-campaign to complement organic traffic and target specific product audiences. Five different ad campaigns were created — each targeting a distinct customer segment. The strategy evolved from broad to specific targeting over time, significantly improving results.

🤝 Affiliate Marketing

🌐 Community & Content Management
Two branded online communities were built to generate organic brand engagement and user-driven content. Gamification features (achievements, challenges, avatars) were added to keep members active and returning. These communities drove referral traffic that grew from 390 to 1,087 monthly visits.

🏅 Key Wins
🌱
Top 3 SEO Performance Out of 42 Businesses
The organic-first content strategy consistently outperformed paid channels. Organic traffic grew 85% — from 848 to 1,570 monthly visitors — making SEO the single most powerful channel in the entire campaign.
🧪
A/B Testing Proved Concise Copy Converts Better
Email A/B testing showed that shorter, direct copy with one clear call-to-action consistently outperformed longer detailed emails on conversions. This finding was rolled out across all future campaigns, improving overall email performance.
💷
£49,146 Revenue from Organic Alone
The vast majority of total revenue — £49,146 out of £50,727 — was generated through organic web traffic and SEO. Zero paid spend was needed to achieve this, proving the long-term ROI of a strong content and SEO foundation.
📈
Impressions Grew 4,225% After Campaign Restructure
Paid ad impressions jumped from 1,185 to 46,811 after removing keyword cannibalisation between campaigns and refocusing each ad on a distinct audience segment. A targeted approach dramatically outperformed the initial broad strategy.
🤝
Community Built 73% Engagement Rate
The peer marketplace community achieved a 73.5% engagement rate — far above industry averages. Community members generated organic referral traffic that grew 179% over the campaign, acting as an always-on, zero-cost marketing channel.
