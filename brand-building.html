01 — Rebranding

A Brand That Speaks
Before You Do

I pitched a strategic rebrand: keeping the established purple but introducing a bold coral/orange accent that would make the brand feel approachable, energetic, and memorable in a traditionally stiff financial services space.

02 — Content Strategy

From Product Posts
to Brand Stories

The Strategic Shift

Previously, the company's social content was entirely product-focused — "Here's our business loan, here's our rate." Functional, but invisible. I introduced two new content pillars that transformed the brand's online presence: Educational Content (making finance topics accessible and shareable) and Brand Storytelling (connecting human stories to financial solutions). Each post was designed, copywritten, and produced using Canva — entirely in-house.

03 — Brand Mascot

Meet the Panda Broker

The World's Most Approachable Finance Broker

Finance is intimidating. Brokers are perceived as stiff, formal, and transactional. I introduced a brand mascot that flipped that perception entirely — a 3D animated Panda dressed as a finance broker, complete with a navy suit, crisp tie, and an orange pocket square nodding to the brand's new palette.

The panda became the face of educational content, social media campaigns, TikTok videos, and email marketing — transforming a B2B financial services firm into a brand with genuine personality. Warm, witty, and surprisingly relatable for an industry that rarely smiles.

I conceived, scripted, directed, shot, and edited a branded video series called the Funding Formula — bringing the director and employees in front of camera for episodic content that blended financial education, fun games, industry insights, and the firm's finance solutions. Each episode had cinematic visual design with bold title treatments, a distinct theme and tone. The series drove organic leads and built genuine audience trust across platforms.
