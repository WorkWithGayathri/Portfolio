Automation Workflows Built

Each workflow was independently designed and built inside HubSpot — eliminating manual steps, reducing lead response time, and keeping the sales pipeline moving automatically.

WORKFLOW 01
Lead Capture Automation
Automatically processes every new inbound lead from the moment a form is submitted — no manual input required.
Landing Page Form Submitted → Contact Created in HubSpot → Lead Status Set to New → Assigned via Round Robin → Automated Welcome Email Sent
WORKFLOW 02
Lead Lifecycle Management
Tracks each lead through the funnel and triggers personalised nurture emails based on behaviour — moving them towards becoming a qualified lead.
Lead Created → Lifecycle Stage Tracked → Behaviour-Triggered Nurture Emails → Score Threshold Met → MQL → Sales Team Notified
WORKFLOW 03
Round Robin Lead Assignment
Distributes new leads evenly across the sales team automatically — ensuring fair workload split and instant follow-up every time.
New Lead Comes In → Round Robin Logic Applied → Owner Assigned to Contact → Follow-Up Task Auto-Created → Rep Notified Instantly
WORKFLOW 04
Deal Pipeline Automation
Keeps deals moving through the pipeline automatically — stage-triggered communications, task reminders, and manager alerts at every milestone.
Deal Created in CRM → Stage-Based Email Triggers → Reminder Tasks Auto-Created → Manager Notified on Movement → Closed Won/Lost Updates Lifecycle
