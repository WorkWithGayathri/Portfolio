02 — What Goes Into One

Anatomy of a
Business Plan

Every plan I write covers these twelve sections — from high-level vision all the way through to risk mitigation and operational detail.

01
Executive Summary
Vision, mission, opportunity in brief
02
Business Overview & Mission
What the business does and why it exists
03
Market Research & Analysis
Industry size, trends, demand evidence
04
Competitor Landscape
Direct & indirect competitors, positioning gaps
05
Target Customer Profile
Demographics, psychographics, buying behaviour
06
Revenue Model & Pricing
How the business makes money, pricing rationale
07
Financial Projections (3-Year)
P&L, cash flow, break-even analysis
08
Funding Requirements
Total ask, loan vs. equity, repayment plan
09
Use of Funds Breakdown
Where every pound goes and why
10
Operations & Staffing Plan
Team structure, roles, day-to-day operations
11
Marketing & Launch Strategy
Go-to-market plan, channels, customer acquisition
12
Risk Assessment & Mitigation
Key risks identified with contingency responses
